Introduction:

Welcome to the Introduction of my Interaction Design project, focused on gamifying a learning app. Our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non game contexts, such as education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagement and enjoyment!

This project involved various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping and usibility testing. The competitive analysis helped me identify strenghts and weaknesses in the existing market and highlight areas where we can improve.

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable learning process

Finally, usabilty testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the app's usibility and functionality. The feedback will be used to refine and improve the app, ensuring that the users needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, ultimately improving the users learning outcomes.
